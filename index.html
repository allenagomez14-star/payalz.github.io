About Me

Bubbly. Bold.
Boundary-free.

I'm a solo traveller, fashion & travel blogger, cloud engineer, model, and anchor who lives life at full throttle — with a laptop in one hand, a passport in the other, and a smile that never quits.

From the canals of Amsterdam to the temples of Bali, cherry blossoms in Japan to the Taj Mahal at sunrise — I've explored 36+ countries solo, proving that a career-driven woman and a globe-trotting free spirit are one and the same.

My Philosophy

"Live so fully that your passport runs out of pages before you run out of dreams."

  • Work hard, travel harder — ambition and adventure fuel each other, not fight each other.
  • Solo travel is the ultimate self-discovery. Every country teaches me something about myself I didn't know I needed to learn.
  • You don't need a partner, a big budget, or permission. You need a passport and a willingness to say yes.
  • Tech and creativity aren't opposites. The best solutions are built by people who see beauty in both code and culture.
  • Be the woman who breaks the mould — career-driven, globally curious, unapologetically herself.
  • Work-life balance is non-negotiable. Rest is productive. Joy is necessary. Living fully is the point.
